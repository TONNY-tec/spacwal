About Us

SpacNwal Services is a registered company under The Companies Act (Chapter 499, section 14 of the Laws of Kenya). We are committed to offering high standards in the cleaning industry, with a strong belief in customer satisfaction and professionalism.

Our team is highly experienced and well-trained. All our products and services are tailored to meet the individual needs of our customers. We specialize in a wide range of services including general office/home cleaning, sanitary bin services, fumigation, garbage disposal, interior décor/design, landscaping, and supply of cleaning and hygiene materials.

Since our inception, SpacNwal Services has maintained an unshakable commitment to our foundation values which guide everything we do.

Our History

Our Director, a graduate in Science with a Masters in Project Management (specializing in environmental projects), began the journey in 2004 as an independent distributor for cleaning products. The quality and demand for these products laid the foundation for the birth of SpacNwal Services.

Her experience in customer service at an Internet Service Provider shaped her vision for a customer-centered cleaning business. Today, SpacNwal Services is reshaping perceptions about cleaning and environmental management.

With plans to launch an online ordering system in partnership with our distributors, our goal is to make accessing cleaning products as easy as a click.
