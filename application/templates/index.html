Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in Cleaning & Environmental Services

SpacNwal Services is a registered company offering high-standard cleaning and environmental solutions in Kenya. Founded by a seasoned expert in science and project management, our journey began in 2004 as a product distributor and has grown into a full-fledged cleaning service provider.

We specialize in general office/home cleaning, fumigation, landscaping, interior decor, garbage management, and hygiene supply.
